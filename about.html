Er. Yomesh Rao, Managing Director. ( B.E.Civil, L.L.B, L.L.M )

‘The Road to success is Always under Construction.’

Yomesh Rao has almost 22 years’ of Operational and Strategic Experience in Real Estate and Construction Industry across India.
With a Bachelors Degree in Engineering, He Started his career as a Site Engineer with the Raheja Group based in Mumbai. His Knowledge and Experience in Technical Due Diligence, Project Approvals and Clearances throughout the lifecycle of Real Estate Projects has gained him considerable respect from Regulatory Authorities.
He is the Chairman of CVSRTA, Presently the Hon. Secretary of the Practising Engineers, Architects and Town Planners Association, Mumbai, Member of Institution of Engineers. Though gentle in Approach, He has a penchant for getting things done, with the Ability to Plan, Strategise and make quick but correct Decisions. When it comes to Construction he’s been there, done that, but is still willing to learn.
Undertaken Projects Initially Limited in Bombay and then Moving in to Pan India Liaison by Studying and Understanding the Local Rules and Regulations of Various Cities like Hyderabad, Bangalore, Mangalore, Chandigarh, Haryana, Chennai, Goa etc……..
Yomesh originally wanted to be a Police officer. He Studied Law as a hobby and is a Qualified LLB and Subsequently Completed his Masters in Law.
In his life he always strives to see justice done, though now, it’s for his Clients.
